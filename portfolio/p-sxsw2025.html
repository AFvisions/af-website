SXSW 2025 – Campus Signage Coordination

Role: Event Signage Coordinator (Large Format)
Tools: Airtable, Adobe Creative Suite, PageProof, Kami
Project Type: Event Ops & Environmental Branding

The Challenge

SXSW 2025 required urgent support for signage production across its vast downtown campus. I joined mid-cycle to help the Experience department manage a rapidly growing workload and ensure seamless brand and wayfinding integration across the Austin Convention Center and surrounding venues.

My Role

I was responsible for designing, coordinating, and managing over 200 signage requests from concept to installation—nearly 4,000 sq. ft. of large-format print materials. These signs supported departments including:

  • Conference Programming
  • Sales & Exhibitions
  • Music, Film & TV

In addition, I provided logistical and production support for the broader signage ecosystem totaling over 15,000 sq. ft. of installed work.

Strategy & Execution

Using Airtable as a centralized project tracker, I managed:

  • A high-volume intake system to organize signage requests by department, location, type, and production schedule
  • The design, proofing, and production of 202 signage pieces — all completed within a 3-week sprint
  • On-site deployment across dozens of venues, including access coordination, installation timing, and breakdown logistics

I built on templates developed by the internal production team, adapting them to meet late-stage needs and shifting requirements. On-site, I helped troubleshoot issues and ensured signage was correctly deployed and maintained throughout the festival.

Results

  • On-brand signage across dozens of venues with minimal issues
  • Attendees experienced little to no wayfinding friction
  • Quick issue resolution on-site ensured smooth operations

Post-Event

I supported invoice reconciliation, sign tracking, and project retrospectives. I also contributed concepts for improving signage in 2026, including early-stage mockups of advanced wayfinding approaches.
