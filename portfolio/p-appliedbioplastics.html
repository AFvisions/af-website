Project Type:

Email Development, Email Marketing, Graphic Design

Software + Tools:

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Figma, HTML + CSS, Litmus, Parcel, VSCode

Problem:

Applied Bioplastics supplies economically sustainable plant-based plastic alternatives supporting eco-friendly agriculture in developing countires. The Founder and Marketing Director wished to discover who of their audience would be interested in becoming early investors in their materials revolution.

Solution:

An Indication of Interest campaign was initiated and set to last for one week. I hand-crafted a template used for both the opening and closing of the campaign utilizing the brand styleguide, a custom-made gif, and following best practices to create a concise and cohesive, responsive template. I tested the template extensively to ensure accessability across dozens of ESPs and optimized for each accordingly utilizing tactics such ghost tables, "bulletproof" buttons, and dark mode optimization.

Impact:

With a focus on engagement I found a high CTR of 8.0% and 10.1% indicating great interest in the company and the campaign. Campaign Monitor 2022 Report

The unsubscribe rate and bounce rate sat quite high at 2.5% and 9.2% respectively, which isn't surprising considering many of the unsubs came from in-person sign ups. It's likely expectations were not set for subscribers via a welcome email, or previous marketing emails.

Overall, Applied Bioplastics reported satisfaction but were unable to tell which conversions came from their own marketing channels or my email campaigns with the exception of those who replied directly to the emails.
